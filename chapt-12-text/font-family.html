Briards

Text with capitalize

by Ivy Duckett

The briard or berger de brie, is a large breed of dog traditionally used as a herder and guardian of sheep. Justified text looks at the words on each individual line and creates an equal gap between those words. It can look odd if you end up with large gaps between some words and smaller gaps between others. This often happens when your lines are not very wide or when your text contains long words.
