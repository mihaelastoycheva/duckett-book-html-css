1. Tools to edit (see page 108 in book)

JPGs, GIFs, and PNGs belong to a type of image format known as bitmap. 
(pixels per inch -> ppi)

Images in print materials (such as books and magazines) are made up of tiny circles 
called dots. (dots per inch -> dpi)

*Images created for the web should be saved at a resolution of 72 ppi.
 Images created for print should be made up at a resolution of 300 dpi.
